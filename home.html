How The Model Works

01

Digital Commerce

Revenue is generated through the consumption of digital products and services. Every day millions of transactions are generated through online services, advertising networks and digital platforms.

02

Advertising Participation

Partners participate in revenue generated through advertising activities and digital marketing campaigns. DIP connects participants with these revenue flows through a structured model.

03

Revenue Sharing

Participants receive a share of the generated revenue streams according to the platform's participation model. This structure allows partners to benefit from the continuous growth of digital markets.

Participants can introduce new partners to the platform, expanding the reach of our ecosystem. For each successful referral, a one-time referral commission may be granted according to platform guidelines.
